"Stop waiting and start living."

Pitch:

GOLINE is an iOS mobile app that helps people reduce their time waiting in line at busy venues for music shows, sport events, product launches, and other events. GOLINE enables users to request line sitters to hold the place, and wait in the lines instead. Anyone who has gotten bored waiting for two hours out in the cold will no longer need to stand in that line.

This project was for HCDE 503: Navigating Design in Organizations.
