The Killjoy Luck Club

About:

"The Killjoy Luck Club" is a podcast founded by three University of Washington graduate students, myself, Dana Lee and Christina Hughes. Produced by Cherlaine Ordona and hosted by Worktank on Capitol Hill in Seattle, we wanted to create a space where we can discuss what it means to be a first and second generation Asian-American feminist in today's world -- as well as issues that surround other marginalized groups.
