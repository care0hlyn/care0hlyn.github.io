##about

I'm a front end developer, user experience designer and graduate student at the University of Washington in the Human Centered Design & Engineering program. My concentration is on user centered design and rapid prototyping -- specializing in both 2D and 3D environments. I am expected to graduate in June 2017.

My ideology in life best aligns with the work of Bertrand Russell. There is no hierarchy between the pursuit of love and knowledge -- and that's how I try to live my life. I am an amateur photographer, traveler (backpacked through India, Mexico), burgeoning yogi and foodie... which leads to my secret life as a co-owner of an instant ramen food cart (@nomnommarket) here in sunny Seattle, WA!

Thanks for stopping by and feel free to reach out at any time!
